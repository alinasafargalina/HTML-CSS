🍳 Eggs B. in SLC 🍳

If like me, when you hear "breakfast", you think "Eggs Benedict", here is a short and very subjective review of a few places in Salt Lake City, Utah where you can have Eggs Benedict. At the end of the page, you will see my own rating of the places described. Enjoy! (This site that I created myself and Eggs Benedict, of course).


OASIS CAFE Menu Phone

Monday - Sunday 8 am - 9 pm


Eggs Benedict Florentine in this cozy place will cost you $11. This includes two poached eggs on English muffins with tomatoes, steamed spinach, and hollandaise sauce. Comes with the side of breakfast potatos. For extra $2 you can add smoked salmon, Canadian bacon, applewood bacon, or avocado. The place is rarely crowded and right in downtown. While waiting for your order, you can stop by a quaint book store that is right next to the restaurant.

FINN'S CAFE Menu Phone

Monday - Sunday 7:30 am - 2:30 pm


For $14.89, you can have two poached eggs and Canadian bacon served on toasted English muffins with Finn's Hollandaise sauce and hash browns. The vegetarion option with tomatoes and avocade will cost you $12.80. This is a very popular place among Utahns and can get quite crowded around breakfast on weekends. If you decide to sit inside, it gets very loud, too.

LITTLE AMERICA HOTEL Menu Phone

Monday - Sunday 6 am - noon


They call it "The European" here, at The Coffee Shop of Ltlle America Hotel, and it will cost you $16. For the price, you will have the two egss classic with homemade hollandaise sauce and cottage-fried potatoes. Not your everyday spot, but great for a special breakfast/brunch.

EGGSBURGH Menu Phone

Monday - Sunday 7 am - 3 pm


Six types of Eggs Benedict with prices ranging between $13.50 and $16.00!!! If the choice is too hard, choose one of each of your two favorite Benedicts for $16.50. It is right in downtown, so parking can get tricky if you decide to go there on a weekday.
